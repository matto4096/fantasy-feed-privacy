# Privacy Policy

**Fantasy Feed** is developed by Matt Oldfield.

This privacy policy applies to the Fantasy Feed app for iOS.

## Data Collection

Fantasy Feed does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not track your activity, and does not use any analytics or advertising frameworks.

## Third-Party Services

Fantasy Feed fetches live AFL match data from publicly available AFL APIs. No personal information is transmitted as part of these requests.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please use the support link on the App Store.
